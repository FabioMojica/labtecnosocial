ABSTRACT
This work develops a web-based system for project management at the Social Technologies Laboratory, a non-profit organization based in Cochabamba, Bolivia. The identified problem is related to the inefficiency of manual processes for strategic and operational planning, monitoring of indicators, and report generation, which leads to delays and errors in decision-making.
The main objective is to design and implement a system that integrates user management, strategic plans, and operational projects, as well as to automate data collection from external APIs such as GitHub, Google Play Console, Google Analytics, and social media platforms.
The applied methodology was based on the adaptation of the Scrum agile framework, with short iterations and incremental deliveries. The results achieved include a backend built with Express.js and TypeORM, a frontend developed in React, a PostgreSQL database, and an interactive dashboard that visualizes key performance indicators (KPIs).
It is concluded that the proposed solution significantly reduces project tracking time, minimizes human errors, and strengthens the organizationâ€™s analytical and decision-making capabilities.
